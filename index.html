About Me

I am an Assistant Professor of Marketing at Bocconi University. Prior to joining Bocconi, I was a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at the Tuck School of Business, Dartmouth College (2023–25) [1].

I earned my Ph.D. in Marketing from the Indian Institute of Management Ahmedabad (IIMA), where I received the Prof. Tirath Gupta Memorial Award for Best Thesis [1].

Research Interests

Focus Areas: Retailing, Consumer Journeys, Digital Marketing, Top Management Teams

Methodology: Econometrics, Machine Learning, Causal Inference
